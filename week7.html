Third Simulated Prototype

Inspired by the DUM-series companion robots deployed in the Star Wars saga, this interface has two basic interaction modalities.


On the one hand, by pressing or hitting it down, the robotic arm sort of kneels at you. On the other hand, the robot can be guided through the pressure of the palm in different directitons.

A Vignette

Now that three prototypes have been made, a scenario can be outlined. First, these prototypes might not be actual end-effector, as they seem more intuitive as desktop controllers. Then the end effector can be anything perhaps.
